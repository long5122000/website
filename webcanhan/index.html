WHO I AM

About

My name is Long and I am Vietnamese. I am twenty years old. I live in Ha Noi City, in the north of Vietnam. I have a small family with three people. My father is a teacher at a middle school. He has worked for 35 years in the field and he was the biggest role model of my life. My mother is a housewife. She is kind and can cook very well. I love my family so much. I'm a generous and easygoing person but when it comes to work, I'm a perfectionist in competition. I'm also an optimistic and outgoing person so I have a lot of friends and other social connections. I love reading, writing and doing math. I decided to become a programmer because I have always been fascinated by the web. A Software Engineer degree allows me to achieve my goals and also gives me the opportunity to make a difference in the community. Five years from now, I want to be a project manager of a software project. As a project manager, my main goals are software management and decision making. I also want to speak English fluently so that I can work with people from different countries.
